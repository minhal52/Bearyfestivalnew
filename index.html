About Our Event

Karnataka Beary's Festival is a three-day extravaganza that showcases the state's rich coastal cultural heritage through diverse programs and concepts. The festival features a wide range of stalls, including food, handicrafts, and cultural exhibitions, offering a unique shopping and dining experience.
