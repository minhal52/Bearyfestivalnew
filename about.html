What is
Karnataka Beary’s Festival?

Karnataka Beary’s Festival is a two-day extravaganza that showcases the state’s rich coastal cultural heritage through diverse programs and concepts. The festival features a wide range of stalls, including food, handicrafts, and cultural exhibitions, offering a unique shopping and dining experience.

Our festival also includes a variety of programs, such as cultural performances, music, dance, and art exhibitions, as well as motivation speeches, awareness programs, games, and sports activities.

Furthermore, the festival provides a platform for career growth and business opportunities, featuring a “Job Mela” and “Career Mela” to connect job seekers with potential employers, and “Business Opportunities” and “Networking Programs” to bring together industry leaders, entrepreneurs, and professionals for meaningful connections and collaborations.

Why
Karnataka Beary’s Festival?

Karnataka Beary’s Festival is a celebration of the state’s rich cultural heritage, aimed at promoting unity, diversity, and inclusivity among its people. The festival seeks to preserve and showcase the unique traditions, customs, and artistic expressions of Karnataka’s coastal community, while also providing a platform for cultural exchange, education, and entertainment.

By highlighting these aspects, the Karnataka Beary’s Festival aims to:

  • Preserve and promote the community’s cultural heritage
  • Foster unity and inclusivity among community members
  • Empower youths and promote leadership development
  • Recognize and celebrate community achievements and contributions
  • Promote awareness and social cohesion
  • Showcase the community’s diversity and richness
